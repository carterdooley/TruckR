    About Us

    TruckR is an application for contract truck drivers. With noticable supply shortages, this application's purpose is to target the truckers who have given up their jobs for unfair pay. Companies are struggling to get cargo across the country with the shortage of truck drivers, but with contract driving: drivers have the ability to choose the journey they want, with the pay they deserve.
